How it works

Your daily dose of tea in 3 simple steps

01

Tell us what you like (and what not)

Never again waste time thinking about what tea to drink! TeaLeaf AI will create a 100% personalized tea recommendations just for you. It makes sure you get the highest quality and suitable flavors, no matter what tea you love!

02

Approve your monthly tea package plan

Once per month, approve the tea package plan generated for you by TeaLeaf AI. You can change ingredients, swap entire tea, or even add your own recipes.

Best delivery services in town will ship your tea each week, and we will deliver it to your door whenever works best for you. You can change delivery schedule and address daily!
